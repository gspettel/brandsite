The way we talk

Don't say

Adjectives become meaningless when they're overused...

Do say

...but a nod to the show says we're fans too and won't confuse people who don't watch it.

Don't say

A functional explanation of our product isn’t spine-tingling...

Do say

...but precise descriptors add excitement and make our products shine.

Fresh, energetic writing that's free from cliché

Craft copy that's lively and confident. Focus on key features, not every feature - that way, the benefits stand out and make us different. Use plenty of verbs so it feels active. Forget about superlatives and clichés. Vary rhythm and pace, and use short, sharp sentences for impact. Really.

Don't say

Our easy-to-use industry-leading On Demand service features state-of-the-art technology and is jam-packed with loads of amazing box sets you’re sure to love.

Clichéd descriptors and listing out all the product features make information hard to digest...

Do say

Missed the series everyone talked about? Mad Men, Boardwalk Empire, The Sopranos... We’ve put the box sets in your Sky+ box.

...but breaking up the information, using short sentences, only focusing on one benefit makes everything feel easier.

Don't say

Our aim is to get more people cycling with Sky Ride. Join us today!

Exclamation marks won’t make dull copy exciting...

Do say

Let’s get Britain back on their bikes. Join us at the next Sky Ride.

...but verbs add energy and make the call to action more inviting.

Concise, conversational and perfectly clear

Talk naturally and use everyday words and phrases; things you’d really say. Leave the technical jargon and formalities behind, keep things clear and to the point. No need to shout. Get contractions in - we’re, it’s, you’re - so it’s easy. Read what you’ve written out loud – if you need to take a breath, the reader will too.

Don't say

We’ve recently streamlined our billing process and implemented a simple new system so that our customers are fully aware of when their bills are due to arrive.

Long sentences and stuffy words can make people lose interest...

Do say

We’ve made things simpler so you’ll always know when to expect your bills.

...but a concise, conversational style flows naturally and gets straight to the point.

Don't say

Get a Sky Movies subscription HALF PRICE for 6 months!

Shouting won’t make an offer sound good...

Do say

Get more of the year’s biggest box office hits for less. Sky Movies is half price for 6 months.

...but giving the customer the benefit in a more human way shows them the value.
